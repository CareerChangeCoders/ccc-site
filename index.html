Why Career Change Coders?

Leaving an established career to pursue a dream of becoming a web developer is a huge decision. Not everyone will understand. It can be a lonely journey at times. We've been there. We wanted to create an active community of like minded career changers to support, learn, share and grow with.

About

From humble beginnings to an international community

  • June 2017

    Let's chat...

    Career Change Coders is born.
    After connecting via email, Rick and David set up a Slack channel to make communication and collaboration on projects easier.

  • Getting Social

    Wanting to spread the word and inspire the next generation of career changers Rick and David set up the Career Change Coders Medium Publication. We're also on facebook and twitter too.

  • A growing family

    The community starts to grow. Web developers and aspiring career changers from all over the world start to join the conversation.
